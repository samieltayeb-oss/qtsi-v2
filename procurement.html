Why Procurement Fails

Without an executive governance layer, organizations leak capital across fragmented ecosystems and unmanaged lifecycles.

Vendor sprawl

Multiple suppliers diluting purchasing leverage and complicating vendor management.

Shelfware

Over-provisioned or completely unused software licensing quietly draining the budget.

Emergency purchasing

Reactive buying at the worst possible price due to lack of lifecycle planning.

Inconsistent standards

Fragmented hardware causing massive IT support overhead and security gaps.

Hidden lifecycle costs

Failing to account for warranties, secure disposal, and transition timelines.
