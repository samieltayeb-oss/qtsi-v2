# QTSI Brand Book v2

## 1. The Design Manifesto
We are **not** building a cybersecurity vendor website.
We are **not** building a managed services website.
We are building the digital presence of an executive advisory firm.

Every page must communicate:
- **Authority**
- **Clarity**
- **Trust**
- **Simplicity**
- **Executive confidence**

> [!WARNING]
> **The Quality Gate**
> - If an element exists only because it "looks cool," it doesn't belong.
> - If an animation distracts from the message, remove it.
> - If a section does not improve trust or conversion, remove it.
> - Every pixel must justify its existence.

---

## 2. Brand Positioning
**Positioning Statement:** *Canada's trusted AI Governance & Executive Cyber Advisory firm.*

QTSI operates at the board level. We do not sell hardware speeds or server configurations on the public-facing brand; we sell risk mitigation, institutional trust, and digital governance.

## 3. Tone of Voice
- **Authoritative but Accessible:** Speak directly, without jargon.
- **Confident:** We know the landscape. We guide the client.
- **Concise:** Executives do not have time for fluff. Every sentence must deliver value.

## 4. Visual Language: "Institutional Light"
- **Backgrounds:** Clean, stark white and subtle off-whites (e.g., `#FFFFFF`, `#FAFAFA`).
- **Typography:** High-contrast, crisp black/dark gray for readability (e.g., `#111827`).
- **Accents:** A disciplined use of a single premium brand color (e.g., a deep, institutional blue or a very specific executive green). No neon.
- **Photography:** Authentic, professional, executive-focused. Avoid generic "hacker in a hoodie" stock imagery. 
- **Iconography:** Minimal, line-based, consistent stroke weights. Not cartoony.

## 5. Typography
- **Primary Font:** *Inter* (or similar clean geometric sans-serif).
- **Hierarchy:** Clear distinction between H1 (Display), H2 (Section Headers), and Body text.
- **Readability:** Maximum line length of 65-75 characters for body text. Generous line-height (1.5 - 1.6).

## 6. Motion & Animation
- **Purpose-Driven:** Motion is only used to direct the eye or confirm an interaction (e.g., a subtle button hover, a clean page transition).
- **Prohibited:** Infinite ambient loops, heavy 3D background renders, or scroll-jacking that impairs reading.

## 7. Performance as a Brand Value
Speed is a signal of quality. Adhere strictly to the established Performance Budget:
- Lighthouse Desktop: ≥ 95
- Lighthouse Mobile: ≥ 90
- LCP: < 2.0 s
- INP: < 200 ms
- CLS: < 0.05
